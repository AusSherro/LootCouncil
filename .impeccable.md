## Design Context

### Users
- Two-person household (the developer and their girlfriend) managing personal finances
- YNAB power users who want full budget visibility without friction
- Context: Daily budgeting, monthly planning, transaction review — used frequently, needs to be fast and pleasant

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, modern, factual — the interface should feel like a reliable financial tool that takes your money seriously while being enjoyable to use regularly.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Polished & premium**: Refined details, intentional spacing, quality feel in every interaction
- **Finance light theme by default**, with six optional personality themes including dark gold-accented "Dungeon"
- **Reference**: YNAB — information-dense but organized, everything visible without digging, easy to scan and act on
- **Fonts**: Geist Sans / Geist Mono
- **No component library**: Custom CSS design system with Tailwind CSS 4

### Emotional Goals
- **Delight & engagement**: Should be enjoyable to use regularly, not a chore
- **Trust**: Numbers must feel accurate and clearly presented
- **Control**: Users should feel on top of their finances at a glance

### Design Principles
1. **Information density over decoration** — Show real data prominently. Every element earns its place.
2. **Hierarchy through spacing and weight** — Use whitespace and font weight as primary tools. Reserve color for meaning (positive/negative/warnings).
3. **Consistent rhythm** — Spacing should follow a defined scale. Tight grouping for related data, generous breathing room between sections.
4. **Premium restraint** — Polish comes from alignment, consistent spacing, and refined details — not from effects, gradients, or ornamentation.
5. **Scannable at a glance** — The most important information should be identifiable within 2 seconds on any page.
